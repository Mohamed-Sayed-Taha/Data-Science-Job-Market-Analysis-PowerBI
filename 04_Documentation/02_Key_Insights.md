## Key Takeaways

### For Job Seekers
1. **Target Mid-Sized Companies** (1,001-5,000) - best balance of pay vs. competition
2. **Master the "Big Three"** - SQL + Python + Cloud appear in 28% of jobs
3. **Consider Staffing Agencies** - 17% of market with competitive pay ($131K)
4. **Data Engineer is the hottest role** - 36.2% market share, $134K average

### For Companies
1. **Small-Medium companies** (51-200) need better value proposition (26 applicants per job!)
2. **Cloud skills are non-negotiable** - demand doubled in 2 years
3. **Internet & IT Services** = 46% of all data jobs - benchmark against these
